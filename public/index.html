About Jaycee

Okpara Jude Chizaram popular known as JAYCEETALKS, is a World Class Event Host , A multi-award winning On-air personality, fashion model, brand influencer, and social media strategist.

He is a radio presenter at Toast90.3fm owerri, and also listed among the top 20 young On-air personalities in Nigeria.

As a Prolific emcee, he has hosted events for top organisations like the JPI ANNUAL LEADERSHIP CONFERENCE 2022, one of the biggest conferences in the southeast, Vantage Heights Capital, the Imo state Governor's dinner party, SMFEST, one of the biggest social media and tech conferences in the southeast twice in a roll and many other events to his credit.

His love for media and fashion with a touch creativity stands him out.

He is Igbo, from Abia state, and can speak other languages like Yoruba and also a little bit of French. He is an impressionist, with the ability to speak in over 7 different accents. He is a humanitarian to the core. As he always seek to add value to anyone he meets. He is one of the best hands in the Media Industry.
